Flowers

Heather (Calluna vulgaris)

Heather is a hardy evergreen shrub known for its tiny purple-pink blooms that create a soft, cloud-like appearance.It symbolizes admiration and good luck, often used in gardens for year-round color and texture.

Milkweed (Asclepias)

Milkweed is known for its star-shaped blooms that attract butterflies—especially monarchs, which rely on it as their main host plant.It’s an important wildflower for pollinators and plays a vital role in supporting healthy ecosystems.

Asiatic Lily

Asiatic lilies are known for their vibrant colors and elegant, star-shaped blooms that appear early in summer.They symbolize purity and renewal and are popular in gardens for their low maintenance and striking beauty.
